Nay Pyi Taw Flood Relief

In September 2024, severe flooding in Nay Pyi Taw affected thousands of people, damaging homes, livelihoods, and entire communities. During this crisis, I actively participated in on-ground relief efforts to support those in need.

Together with a team, we prepared and distributed essential supplies such as food, water, and daily necessities to affected families. I was involved in coordinating distribution, assisting with logistics, and directly engaging with communities impacted by the disaster.

This experience strengthened my commitment to helping others. It showed me the importance of teamwork, compassion, and being present during difficult times.

My journey in community support began earlier. In 2017 and 2018, together with my friends, I regularly visited communities to support children and elderly people through small donation and assistance efforts. Although we were not able to continue as actively over time, the values and compassion I developed during those years have stayed with me.

I continue to help others in any way I can, and I carry a strong desire to do more. I want to be part of a community that creates meaningful impact and makes life easier for those in need. I am motivated to contribute my skills, time, and dedication to organizations that are committed to helping people.
